About

I've been at SUNY Old Westbury since 2011. I received a B.S. in Math from MIT in 2003, and my Ph.D. in Math from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in 2008. My Ph.D. thesis advisor was Prof. Karen Smith. After that I did a post-doc at University of Missouri-Columbia from 2008-2011, mentored by Prof. Dan Edidin.
